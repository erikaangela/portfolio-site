About

As a pure math grad, I was attracted to the logical, step-by-step process in coding that reminded me of proofs and calculations. It's satisfying seeing how everything comes together by understanding each component's use and effect on another—like using theorems! I like the intention behind coding.

Outside of code, I like to read, write, spend time with friends and family, and search about interior design.
